SocialConnect is an open source protocol that maps off-chain personal identifiers (such as phone numbers, twitter handles, etc.) to on-chain account addresses.

This enables a convenient and interoperable user experience for use cases such as:

- **Payments** - Send money directly to your friend's phone number!
- **Social Discovery** - Find someone's account based on their twitter and any other identity applications!
